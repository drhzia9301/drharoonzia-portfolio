About Me

Hello, it's a pleasure to meet you.

I'm Haroon, a fourth-year medical student at Saidu Medical College. For me, medicine isn't just a career path; it’s a calling to connect with people, understand their stories, and help alleviate suffering. It’s a field that demands both intellectual rigor and deep empathy, and I find that balance incredibly fulfilling.

I'm particularly drawn to cardiology and surgical specialties, but I'm also fascinated by how technology is shaping the future of healthcare. I believe the best doctors are those who never stop learning, and I'm always looking for opportunities to grow—whether in the clinic, through research, or in conversation with others.

When I'm not studying, you might find me lost in a book or trying my hand at writing poetry. This website is a small window into my world, and I'm glad you're here.
