About Me

Dr Haroon Zia

4th Year Medical Student

Hello! I'm Dr Haroon Zia, a dedicated and passionate 4th-year medical student at Saidu Medical College, Swat. My journey into medicine is driven by a profound desire to alleviate suffering and connect with patients on a human level.

Throughout my medical education, I've developed a keen interest in surgical specialties and medical innovation. I thrive in collaborative environments and am constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow, both clinically and personally.

This website is my personal space to document my experiences, share my thoughts on various aspects of medicine and healthcare, and connect with fellow students, mentors, and anyone interested in the medical field. My goal is to become a compassionate and skilled Cardiologist.

Outside of my studies and clinical rotations, I enjoy reading and writing poetry.
